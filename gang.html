  • NAME: Walter Hartwell White
  • AGE: 50
  • OCCUPATION: High School Chemistry Teacher, Carwash Employee
  • FIRST APPEARANCE: S01E01 (Pilot)
  • PORTRAYED BY: Bryan Cranston

"I am the one who knocks!"

Walter White is a loving husband and father. He is a highschool chemistry teacher and also has a sidejob as a carwash employee. On his 50th birthday he collapses and is diagnosed with an inoperable type of lungcancer. Instead of counting down the days he starts producing crystal meth to make a lot of money for his family before his death. His background in chemistry makes him an exceptionally good cook. His signature blue meth becomes so popular that Walt's alias, Heisenberg, becomes an infamous name in all of the USA.

  • NAME: Skyler White
  • AGE: 40
  • OCCUPATION: Bookkeeper, Car Wash Manager
  • FIRST APPEARANCE: S01E01 (Pilot)
  • PORTRAYED BY: Anna Gunn

"Someone has to protect this family from the man who protects this family."

Skyler White, wife of Walter White. Skyler notices Walter behaving strangely and suspects that he is hiding something from her. When she confronts him about it Walt ultimately gives in and explains that he has lungcancer and says that's the reason for his strange behavior. Later Skyler finds out about Walt's real secret, his illegal activities, but does not want to report her husband to the police because she's scared it will ruin her entire family. She has no other choice but to accept Walt's action and even aid him in his quest for money.

  • OCCUPATION: DEA Agent
  • FIRST APPEARANCE: S01E01 (Pilot)
  • PORTRAYED BY: Dean Norris

"My name is ASAC Schrader. And you can go fuck yourself."

Hank Schrader works as a Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) agent. He is also Walter's brother-in-law. Married to Skyler's sister Marie. Hank has an incredible passion for policing and in his career sees himself climbing through the ranks to eventually become ASAC, running his own force. As DEA officer on of his largest investigations into an evergrowing big-roller in the drug scene; Heisenberg.

  • NAME: James "Saul Goodman" Morgan McGill
  • AGE: 48
  • OCCUPATION: Criminal Defense Attorney
  • FIRST APPEARANCE: S02E08 (Better Call Saul)
  • PORTRAYED BY: Bob Odenkirk

"It's the way of the world, kid. Go with the winner."

James Morgan McGill, better known as Jimmy, even better known as Saul Goodman, attorney at law. If you run into any issues involving the law Saul's your guy. With his many contacts Saul always has "a guy" he can call. Don't let his flashy suits fool you, when it comes to it Saul can be the serious attorney you need. With an extensive knowledge of the law (and its many loopholes) he can talk his way out of almost any tricky situation.

  • NAME: Gustavo Fring
  • AGE: 49
  • OCCUPATION: Los Pollos Hermanos Co-Founder, Drug Kingpin
  • FIRST APPEARANCE: S02E11 (Mandala)
  • PORTRAYED BY: Giancarlo Esposito

"What does a man do, Walter? A man provides for his family."

The Chilean Gustavo Fring is known for being co-founder of the fast food restaurant chain Los Pollos Hermanos. But the restaurant is not the only business Gustavo is running. He uses his restaurant as a means to distribute drugs, making him one of the biggest druglords north of the border. When Walt's product starts to compete with Gus', he asks Walt to produce for him, offering him a very large sum of money in return.

  • NAME: Michael Ehrmantraut
  • AGE: 65
  • OCCUPATION: Private Investigator, Head of Corporate Security
  • FIRST APPEARANCE: S02E13 (ABQ)
  • PORTRAYED BY: Jonathan Banks

"I assure you I could kill you from way over here, if it makes you feel any better."

Former police officer Michael Ehrmentraut now works as head of corporate security. However, this job is a cover for Mike's real position, namely Gustavo Fring's right hand man. He also does odd jobs on the side, for example investigative work for Saul Goodman. Mike is in the criminal world for a similar reason to Walter, to make money for his family.
